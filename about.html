About Us

Mak Tech solutions was established in 2019 as an organisation providing solutions for water treatment related problems. We are one of the leading Manufacturer and Service Provider of an extensive array of MTS range Chemicals like Water Treatment Chemicals for Boiler Water & Cooling Tower, Polymers, Culture for STP/ETP DM & RO Plant Chemicals, Polymer DCDA, Effluent Decolouriser, Textile De-Colouriser, Resins and Equipments like fully automated water treatment plants, distillation column trainer, absorption column trainer, interacting and non interacting level trainer, heat exchanger trainer and maintenance of boilers, heat exchangers, condensers, AHUs, control valves, RO plants etc.
